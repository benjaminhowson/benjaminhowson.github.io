Power Calculator: Interactive Sizing Tool Traditional randomised trials require sizing. This calculator allows you to specify: the direction of the change under the alternative hypothesis, your false positive rate, and the magnitude of the effect size. Then, you can play around with sample sizes and variances to discover the statistical power of your design. Remember to do this before running your experiment!
