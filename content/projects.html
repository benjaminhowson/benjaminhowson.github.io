This space is to encourage myself to keep better track of my messy handwritten notes and to convert scripts into interactive web applications.

Play Bandits Play Bandits is an interactive game where you must exercise your ability to make decisions in uncertain environments. Explore. Exploit. See whether your intuition can outsmart the provably efficient algorithms developed over the past century.
Power Calculator for the Z-Test How many samples I you need? What is the smallest effect I can detect? What power do I get with this design? The Z-Test power function allows you to answer all of these questions. Just tweak the inputs until you are happy with your experimental design.
